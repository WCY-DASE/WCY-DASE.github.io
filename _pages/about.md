<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

Dr. Chunyang Wang is currently an Assistant Professor (Chenhui Scholar) at the [School of Data Science and Engineering](https://dase.ecnu.edu.cn/main.htm), East China Normal University (ECNU). He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. [Yanmin Zhu](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/~yzhu/), and was a visiting Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU Singapore) in 2022–2023, under the the supervision of Prof. [Aixin Sun](https://personal.ntu.edu.sg/axsun/index.html). He received his B.Eng from the Faculty of Computing, Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).

<div class="quote-accent">
My research interests focuses on Congitive Computing on <span class="primary-gradient-text">Recommendtion Systems</span>, <span class="primary-gradient-text">Educational Data Mining</span>  and <span class="primary-gradient-text">Language Education Technology</span>, within online platforms in social, e-commerce and educational domains.
<br>
 Looking for highly self-motivated students【Master & Undergraduate】. Contact me <i class='fa fa-envelope'></i> cywang@dase.ecnu.edu.cn.
</div>
